# blinkit-power-bi-project
# 🛒 Blinkit Sales Analysis Dashboard (2022)

An end-to-end Business Intelligence (BI) and Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) project analyzing operational performance, sales optimization, and strategic growth insights for **Blinkit** (formerly Grofers), one of India's leading quick-commerce platforms. This repository contains interactive **Power BI** dashboards, clean transactional data, and an executive presentation detailing consumer preferences and revenue patterns.

## 📌 Project Overview
The primary objective of this analysis is to evaluate sales performance metrics across diverse outlet tiers, sizes, and item categories to uncover high-demand segments and operational bottlenecks. By transforming raw transactional logs into a structured visual model, this project equips stakeholders with data-driven recommendations to maximize profitability and streamline inventory management.

---

## 📊 Core Business Insights & Metrics Tracked

The dynamic dashboard monitors performance across several operational pillars:

### 📈 1. High-Level Executive Overview
* **Total Sales Revenue:** Exceeded **$1.20M** in total sales, reflecting strong platform fundamentals.
* **Volume Transacted:** Over **9,000 items** were successfully ordered and delivered across various regions.
* **Customer Satisfaction:** Holds an average rating baseline of **3.92**, showing high service reliability and consumer trust.

### 🥬 2. Category & Product Demand
* **Revenue Engines:** **Fruits & Vegetables** and **Snack Foods** lead the inventory as primary revenue drivers, tied for top performance at **$180K ($0.18M)** each in total sales.
* **Fat Content Preferences:** Market demand leans significantly toward **Regular Fat** products over Low-Fat variants across all geographic outlet tiers.

### 🏪 3. Outlet Distribution & Timeline Dynamics
* **Size Impact:** **Medium-sized outlets** dominate the format share with **$507.9K** in sales due to an optimized balance between item depth and logistics. Small formats remain highly competitive at **$444.8K**, while High-format stores contribute **$249K**.
* **Type Performance:** **Supermarket Type 1** serves as the primary distribution channel with **$787.5K** in sales volume across 5,577 items.
* **Historical Timeline:** Following an aggressive expansion peak in **2015** (reaching $205K in establishment sales), the platform stabilized into a healthy **$131K baseline by 2022**.

### 📍 4. Geographic & Tier Segmentation
* **Tier 3 Locations:** Lead the market with **$472.13K** in sales, indicating highly successful market penetration into broader urban and suburban zones.
* **Tier 2 ($393K)** and **Tier 1 ($336K)** regions closely follow, establishing a balanced footprint across different population densities.

## 📁 Repository Structure

* **`BlinkIT Grocery Data.xlsx - BlinkIT Grocery Data.csv`**: The clean source dataset containing product attributes, outlet variables, rankings, and sales figures.
* **`dash.pbix`**: The master Power BI Desktop workspace file containing the relational data model, custom DAX tracking measures, and layout design.
* **`Blinkit Sales Analysis 2022.pptx`**: An executive presentation deck translating dashboard visual matrices into strategic growth takeaways and business insights.
